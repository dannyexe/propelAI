Refund Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with PropelAI. If you are not happy with your purchase, we offer a straightforward refund policy outlined below.

1. Free Trial

PropelAI offers a free tier that allows you to generate up to 3 proposals before subscribing. We encourage you to try the free tier to evaluate whether PropelAI meets your needs before upgrading to Pro.

2. Subscription Refunds

If you are not satisfied with your Pro subscription, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial purchase. To request a refund:

  • Email us at support@propelai.app within 7 days of purchase
  • Include your registered email address and reason for the refund
  • We will process your refund within 5–10 business days

3. Renewal Refunds

For monthly subscription renewals, refund requests must be made within 48 hours of the renewal charge. After 48 hours, renewal charges are non-refundable but you may cancel to prevent future charges.

4. How to Cancel

You can cancel your subscription at any time by contacting us at support@propelai.app. After cancellation, you will retain Pro access until the end of your current billing period. You will not be charged again after cancellation.

5. Exceptions

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Requests made after the 7-day refund window for initial purchases
  • Accounts found to be in violation of our Terms of Service
  • Requests citing reasons outside of PropelAI's control (e.g. AI model availability)

6. Contact Us

To request a refund or ask questions about our refund policy, please contact us at support@propelai.app. We aim to respond to all refund requests within 24 hours.

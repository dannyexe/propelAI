Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

At PropelAI, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Email address — used to identify your account and verify Pro status
  • Usage data — number of proposals generated, stored to enforce free tier limits
  • Job postings you submit — processed to generate proposals, not stored permanently
  • Profile information — name, skills, experience you optionally provide to personalize proposals

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the PropelAI service
  • Verify subscription status and enforce usage limits
  • Send important service updates (no marketing without consent)
  • Prevent abuse of the platform

3. AI Processing

Job postings and profile information you submit are sent to Google's Gemini AI API for proposal generation. This data is processed according to Google's privacy policy. We do not permanently store the content of job postings after generation is complete.

4. Data Storage

Your email address and usage count are stored securely in our database hosted by Supabase. Profile information is stored locally in your browser. We do not sell your data to third parties.

5. Payment Information

Payments are processed by Paddle. We do not store your credit card or payment information directly. Please refer to Paddle's privacy policy for details on how payment data is handled.

6. Cookies

We use localStorage in your browser to store your profile and session preferences. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of any communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@propelai.app.

8. Data Retention

We retain your email and usage data for as long as your account is active. If you cancel your subscription and request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at support@propelai.app.
